Behavioural effect can be caused by drugs 

        
            
1) Impaired Coordination: Many drugs can lead to reduced motor skills and coordination.

2) Increased Sociability: Some drugs can lead to increased talkativeness and social 
   interactio.

3) Aggression and Irritability: Certain drugs, especially stimulants and alcohol, can lead 
   to aggressive or irritable behavior.

4) Impulsivity: Drug use can lead to impulsive actions and decisions.
